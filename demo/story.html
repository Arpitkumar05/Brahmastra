On 29th September 2024 I felt that I had to make one Game!

Felt ? Because I never liked games , a few years ago I played games with some college friends for some time but that was just the effect of the company !

On 2 October 2024 I started writing the game. Whatever was coming to mind, I was writing it without stopping !

I named the game GAME OF DOM ( G - O - D ) !

At 4 am on 3rd Oct 2024 , I got a thought that the game I have written is named as GOD, before this I was reading G - O - D !

It was not a big deal, but I was thinking for about half an hour that " something is wrong , something is wrong " !

On 3rd Oct I changed the name of the game, because I was not able to sleep properly because of that name !

And named the game GAME OF GOD ( G O G ) !

Designed a map for the game , named it LAND OF BHARAT ( LOB ) !

Wrote the story of the origin of the map , named it THE ORIGIN OF LOB !

THE ORIGIN OF LOB was related to the incident of the previous night when the word GOD had troubled me !

I met an old man in that dimension, how it happened - what happened, all this is written in THE ORIGIN OF LOB !

When I was writing the conversation between me and that old man, I was writing my name as B in the diary !

That's where Dimension B started !

Now I was writing a game that would be just virtual !

Then I wrote different chapters of the game , like the first phase of GOG will be The Mystery !

I was continuously writing for GOG from 3rd Oct to 10th Oct!

I used to write without stopping whenever I got time !
